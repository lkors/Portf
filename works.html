Smartmatch

December 2015

Smartmatch is a social app concept for finding matches. The idea was developed in a small team of 2 brilliant business-minds and a developer. My main contribution was in defining interaction models and UI specifications. As a result I created a clickable prototype using Marvelapp.

Let’s say that you’re looking for a babysitter. You post your wish on Smartmatch and someone contacts you! Or maybe you’re in the mood for a picnic and you’re looking for people in a similar mood to join you. Whatever’s in your mind, you can always find a smartmatch.

EPSY

July 2016, European Innovation Academy Turin

This project was created during a three-week extreme startup accelerator in Turin, Italy. With the help of two psychotherapists, we developed a working website to provide online psychological counseling. For EPSY I created visual brand guidelines, printables, graphics for social media. I was also responsible for responsive user interface design and front-end development. The main aspect to consider while designing for EPSY was trustworthiness and privacy.

This is a website for providing easy access to psychological counseling through real-time video calls. Connects a wide range of certified psychologists to choose from.

Jaunter

March 2016

The design is made for a mobile version of a website that was created at European Innovation Academy 2015. Based on their CVI I experimented with different designs and created interfaces for the mobile view. App itself is very easy to use, the challenge with this project was developing a smooth onboarding process.

The app lets you find locals with same interests as you when you’re traveling. You just have to enter your hobbies/ interests and the app finds you great company abroad.

AEROC

July 2016

Redesign of AEROC's Estonian website for all devices based on their brand colors. They offer a very wide range of products, thus grouping them to categories was the most reasonable way to present them.

AEROC is a group that sells autoclaved aerated concrete (AAC) products in all Baltic and Nordic countries.Group operates two factories in Estonia and in Latvia.

Nifty.events

July 2015, European Innovation Academy Nice

Developing a brand for the machine-learning event recommendation website Nifty.events. Responsibilities included analysing user workflows, developing website concept, creating visual style guides, defining social media strategy and copywriting.



Nifty.events helps outgoing people spend less time on finding events to their liking. Using complex filters and searches to find events is often frustrating. Nifty solves that issue by applying machine learning algorithms developed at the University of Cambridge to recommend events based on the users likes and dislikes. Business and development: Kristjan Kolde, Marek Rei, Erik Hirmo. Nifty.events won the award of ‘Excellence in Design’ at EIA 2015.
